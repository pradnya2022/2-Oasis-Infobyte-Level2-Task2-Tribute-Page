Bharatratna Dr. B. R. Ambedkar

The principal architect of the Constitution of India and a founding father of the Republic of India.

Great Time Line of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar's Life:

  • 1891- Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar born at Mahu in Madhya Pradesh.
  • 1907- Married to nine year old Ramabai.
  • 1907- Passed matriculation examination from Elphinstone High School, Bombay.
  • 1912- Passed B. A. from Elphinstone College, Bombay.
  • 1913- Leaves for the U.S.A. to join Columbia University, New York.
  • 1915- Awarded M.A. degree in Economics.
  • 1916-Awarded Doctorate in Philosophy degree.
  • 1920-Teaches at Syndenham College of Commerce & Economics, Bombay.
  • 1920-Started “ Muknayak ” Magzine.
  • 1921-Awarded M. S. degree.
  • 1922-Awarded Bar-at-law Degree.
  • 1923-Awarded D.Sc. Degree & called to the Bar.
  • 1924-Starts legal practice at the Bombay High Court.
  • 1924-Launches Bahiskrit Hitkarini Sabha.
  • 1927- Led Chavadar Tale Satryagraha at Mahad.
  • 1942- Forms All India Scheduled Castes Federation.
  • 1945- Starts People's Education Society.
  • 1949- Presents the Draft Constitution in the Constituent Assembly.
  • 1949-Accepted Indian Constitution.
  • 1950- Established Milind College.
  • 1951-Introduces Hindu Code Bill in the Parliament.
  • 1952-Nominated by Bombay Legislature to the Rajya Sabha.
  • 1952-The Columbia University conferred the honorary Degree of Doctor of Laws
  • 1954-Leaves for Rangoon for third World Buddhist Conference.
  • 1956-Embraced Buddhism at Nagpur.
  • 1956-Dies in sleep at Delhi.
  • 1990-Awarded the Bharat Ratna Award.
